Our Founders
Rakhat

After completing over 3 summer schools in the UK, I was sent to the UK to pursue general education att he age of 13. This meant I had to adapt to the new system and prepare myself for the GCSE’s even though the idea was completely out of the ordinary to me, which then followed with A-Levels. Both of these secondary school examinations were new tome, thus guidance was required, but by doing a lot of research and asking for advice from my teachersI managed to be successful in attaining excellent grades! This allowed me to get into a top university, University of Warwick, which is also where I met Iurii.

Over my time at Warwick I completed both a Bachelors and a Masters, however then came a new challenge, in securing a job! Even with top grades and university qualifications, it is extremely competitive in the UK, due to vast talent. However, through perseverance I managed to become a Business Analyst at a SaaS firm, from there my career moved very quickly and I had decided that I wish to share my knowledge with the new generation by opening up Academ Guards!

Iurii

I was only 11 years of age when my academic journey in the UK began. The starting point was Holme Grange, a day school based in Wokingham. Closer to GCSE years, we upgraded to LVS in Royal Ascot, where I had spent a good 2 years. Aiming even higher, my family came across Malvern College. A prestigious coeducational boarding and day school in Worcestershire, the foundation of which dates as far back as 1865. It was a great experience where I grew in mind and character and where I gained excellent preparation for my GCSEs and A-levels. Upon successful completion of secondary education, I was accepted into Warwick University where I had both my BA and MSc degrees.

A bald end to my academy career was an exciting dissertation project where I researched and proposed a market creation for Individually Tradable Pollution Permits. Having worked for 2 years in the Tech industry and gained British Citizenship, I decided to move on to founding Academ Guards with Rakhat, my close friend from Warwick Uni. Today we pride ourselves in working with families from all over the world. We provide A-Z guidance in building your child's prosperous future on British soil covering anything from kindergarten, school, university, employment, and citizenship!
