About us

Our founders, Rakhat from Kazakhstan and Iurii from Armenia, bring a unique blend of Eastern European roots and extensive experience within the UK system. Over the last 15 years, they’ve navigated every aspect of UK life, from school and university to work and immigration. This diverse background allows us to honor our origins while leveraging our UK experiences to provide flexible, tailored solutions to any of your questions or concerns about life in the UK.
