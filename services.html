Services

Visa Application

We offer visa application assistance for students who want tostudy in the UK. Our team can guide students through theapplication process, ensuring that all necessary documents aresubmitted and deadlines are met. We understand that the visaapplication process can be overwhelming, and we are here to helpmake it as smooth as possible.

Guardianship

Our guardianship service provides international students and theirfamilies with peace of mind while studying in the UK. Weunderstand that being away from home can be daunting, which iswhy we offer a comprehensive support system to ensure that ourstudents are well-cared for.

Summer Schools

Our summer school services provide international students with aunique opportunity to experience the UK education system andculture during the summer months. Our programs offer a range ofcourses and activities that are designed to be both educationaland fun. Students will have the opportunity to improve theirEnglish language skills, learn about British culture, and developnew interests and hobbies.

School Admissions

The school admissions service offered by Academ Guards isdesigned to provide international students with the necessaryguidance to secure a place at a UK school that meets theiracademic and personal goals. With a deep understanding of theUK education system, we work closely with schools to ensure thatour students are matched with the right institution.

Tutoring

Our tutoring service provides students with one-on-one academicsupport from experienced and qualified teachers. Whether astudent needs help with a specific subject or wants to improvetheir overall academic performance, our team of tutors is here tohelp. We carefully select our tutors based on their expertise andexperience, ensuring that our students receive the highest qualityinstruction.

Transportation

Moving to a new country can be a daunting experience, especiallyfor young students. At Academ Guards, we understand theimportance of providing comprehensive support to ensure asmooth transition for our students into their new schoolenvironment. Our transportation service provides safe and reliabletransportation for our students to and from school, as well as toany extracurricular activities or appointments

Strive to make your journey to the UK smooth and safe.
